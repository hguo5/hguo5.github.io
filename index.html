Hi I'm Hui Guo

PhD student, Department of Computer Science, North Carolina State University

I am a PhD student majoring in Computer Science at NC State University, under the advisement of Dr. Munindar P. Singh. I am working as a research assistant supported by NSA Science of Security Lablet. Currently, I work in the Multiagent Systems and Service-Oriented Computing Lab.

I received a Master's degree of Science in Computer Science in 2012 from East Carolina Univeristy (Greenville, NC), under the advisement of Dr. Qin Ding. Before that, I received two Bachelor's degrees, Automation Engineering and Economics, in 2009 from Tsinghua University (Beijing, China).

My research interests include natural language processing, data and text mining, multi-agent systems, and social computing. I am currently working on my thesis on event inference.
