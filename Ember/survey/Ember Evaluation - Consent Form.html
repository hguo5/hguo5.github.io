What is the purpose of this study?

The purpose of the study is to understand the gaps (1) between security policies and real breaches, and (2) between human performance and an automated method in extracting useful information from breach reports in the healthcare domain.

What will happen if you take part in the study?

If you agree to participate in this study, you will be asked to

1. Read a tutorial on HIPAA and breach reports;

2. Read five sets of keywords and actions related to data breaches;

3. Identify whether the actions are causally relevant to the keywords;

4. Identify whether the actions are helpful toward HIPAA compliance;

5. Read five breach reports;

6. Specify the useful actions toward the prevention or compensation of the breaches;

7. Complete the post-study survey.

The total duration of the study is 60 minutes.

Risks and Benefits

There are no foreseeable risks associated with participation in this research. The direct benefit to your participation in the research is that you will learn about HIPAA and data breaches in the healthcare domain.
